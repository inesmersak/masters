To apply, you need to:

  • fill out the application form
  • write a short CV (1 page maximum)
  • get a notarized copy of your degree

Send your application to:

Department of Mathematics, Office 3.03
Faculty for Mathematics and Physics
Jadranska 21
1000 Ljubljana
Slovenia

We have to receive your application by September 1, 2014. If you are worried that your application might miss the deadline, send it by registered mail; in that case, it has to be postmarked by September 1, 2014.

E-mail masters@fmf.uni-lj.si if you need more information about the programme, scholarships, tuition, waivers or costs, and with any other questions.
