A Master's Degree in Mathematics is an excellent career choice, whether you want to pursue a PhD
in Mathematics and a career in research and academia, or if you want to find a job in the industry.

Programmes offered

We offer three Master's degree programmes, all of which consist of a number of (mostly elective) courses and a Master's thesis. To enrol, you need a first cycle Bologna degree (or equivalent) in Mathematics or a related field.

Our core programme is Master's Programme in Mathematics. This is the best choice for students with an interest in pure mathematics, for those who wish to pursue a PhD degree, and for those who want maximal flexibility in the selection of courses.

If you want to work in the financial industry, your best choice might be our Master's Programme in Financial Mathematics. In addition to courses in Probability, Statistics and Financial Mathematics, students also take some courses at the Faculty of Economics and some pure mathematics courses.

For students with a pronounced interest in computer science, we recommend Master's Programme in Computer Science and Mathematics. This is a programme run jointly by our department and the Faculty of Computer and Information Science.

Are you a good match for the programme?

We realise that you probably don't speak Slovene, and that two years is a short time to master a language. There is, however, nothing to worry about. All our professors speak English, and will lecture in English if there are students in class who don't speak Slovene. Living in Ljubljana, you will also easily get by with little or no Slovene.

Our programme is of high quality and quite demanding, so you need to have motivation and a proper background to do well. If your undergraduate preparation is not sufficient, you may have to pass additional courses before you can enrol, so do send us an e-mail if you are unsure.
